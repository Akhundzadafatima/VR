What is meant by an Study Zone?
The term "Study zone" refers to an area where we can study separately from the classroom. This area is designed for students to develop their work and study skills. The term "study zone" can be used in a variety of contexts.
Digital space:
In online learning platforms, a digital space where students can study or concentrate. This can be an educational program, a virtual classroom, or a website offering online resources.
Physical space:
A quiet and comfortable environment where students can study. For example, a library, a designated study room at school, or a corner at home can be considered a "study zone."
Mental state:
"Study zone" can also refer to a mental state where students are focused on learning and distanced from external factors. It can also be used in the sense of "attention" or "deep work." In short, a "study zone" is an area or situation that supports focus on learning.
